Plant Helper Terminal

Welcome to the Plant Helper Terminal

This terminal allows you to interact with the Plant Helper system, which monitors and assists in taking care of your plants.

Use the commands below to view and manage the settings of the Plant Helper:

                    
Dirt Moisture Sensor:
  - Current Value: 0.08
  - Max Value: 8000
  - Min Value: 80
  - Emergency Minimum Value: 9

Temperature Sensor:
  - Current Value: 0.06
  - Max Value: 6500
  - Min Value: 65

Humidity Sensor:
  - Current Value: 0.04
  - Max Value: 4000
  - Min Value: 40

Light Sensor:
  - Current Value: 0.02
  - Max Value: 2000
  - Min Value: 20

Commands:
- View settings: view [sensor name] settings
- Update settings: update [sensor name] [parameter] [value]
- Start watering: start watering
- Stop watering: stop watering
